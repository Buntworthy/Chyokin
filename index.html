Daily saving

Small acts of willpower help you save a little bit at a time: not buying that treat, choosing a cheaper option, or finding a better deal. All these virtuous actions cost will power, and the small payoffs get forgotten over time.

Chyokin helps you keep track of how much you save (and when you waste), to see the benefits of being disciplined stacking up towards your saving goal.
